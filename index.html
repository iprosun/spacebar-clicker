Is Your Spacebar Too Noisy?

Mechanical keyboards can be quite noisy, which might be distracting if you prefer a silent environment. But don't worry; we have a solution! Use an Outemu Silent White switch for your spacebar keycap and lubricate it with krytox 205g0 lubricant. These switches are designed to reduce noise, providing a smoother and quieter keystroke experience.

To avoid noisy spacebar taps, lubricate the stabilizer and wire with krytox 205g0 and superlube. If your spacebar becomes loose due to heavy use, fill the gap between the stabilizer with a small piece of cloth or rubber material to fix the rattling.
